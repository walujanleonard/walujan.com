On 5 August 2023, me and my friends going to the waterfall named "Rok Rok Kembes Waterfall". The day before, I planned this to some of my friends to accompany me to go there because I had never been to the place and wanted to go there. I discussed with some friends about what time we could go there the next day, and my friend said around 11am we would go there.

I woke up around half past 11am and started going there around half past 12pm, which we should have left at 11am because I had a little hitch. I went there with my friend on a motorcycle so that we could get there faster and not get tired easily.

On the way, we passed the highway and then started to enter the hills. A few minutes later, we were on the hill and started to enter the way to the waterfall that had been opened long ago to make it easier to get to our destination.

However, my friend had to park his motorcycle when we got close to our destination because the road was not convenient for the motorcycle that our friend was using, so we had to walk. Less than 10 minutes of walking and we finally reached our destination, we arrived at our destination around 12pm.

When I was at the waterfall, I felt like I was in a dream world because the waterfall was so vast and taller than any other waterfall place I had visited a few years ago. Then, I started taking some photos for my own memories. After taking some photos, I started talking with my friend while looking at the beautiful view of the waterfall.

Around half past 3pm, after enjoying the beauty of nature, we started heading home. Finally, I was able to take a moment at the waterfall for the first time.
