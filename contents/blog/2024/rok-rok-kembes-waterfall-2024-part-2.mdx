On 16 June 2024, I was back again on vacation at Rok Rok Kembes Waterfall with my friend. This is the third time I documenting this place which will make some changes for me in documenting at the waterfall because it happened that a month ago I got a new phone, the Samsung Galaxy A55 5G which was released this year. The main advantage of this phone is the IP67 certification which can make me more courageous to take photos near the waterfall and go inside the waterfall to provide unique documentation results.

At half past one in the afternoon, I went to the waterfall with my friend in cloudy and rainy weather conditions. I took this moment in those weather conditions because there were several factors, especially the lighting. When we arrived at our destination, I documented the waterfall at a long distance first. A few minutes later, I immediately took documentation of the waterfall at a close distance, because my mobile phone can withstand water for a certain time, so I decided to take documentation and also I did not forget to go inside the waterfall to take even more unique documentation. After that, I was finally able to get some moments near the waterfall when my mobile phone still safe.
